Info about us

Printing company In Vilvoorde, Flemish Brabant and far beyond, our company is known for its textile and paper printing and has been around for almost 20 years. We are specialized in the production of commercial printing, posters, flyers and much more. On screen printing surface we print textiles, stickers, etc.

Affordable Because we produce everything ourselves, our prices are very competitive and our delivery times are short to very short if necessary.

More Products We supply more graphic products than are listed on this site. If you are looking for a product and you cannot find it here, we can still help you and advise you if necessary.

Expert Advice If you need help with your order or have questions about submitting files, you can always contact us or send an email to dupress@skynet.be . We are happy to help you!

Design Studio In addition to our extensive printing department, we have a design studio to provide you with advice and assistance designing your corporate identity with company logo and promotional material such as desk pads, calendars, presentation folders, notepads, etc.
